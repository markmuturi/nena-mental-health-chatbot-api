Nena Mental Health

Compassionate mental health support, right in your pocket

Nena is an intelligent mental health companion that brings personalized emotional support directly to your WhatsApp. Powered by advanced Dialogflow technology, Nena understands your unique needs and responds with thoughtful, mental health-focused guidance tailored just for you.

Whether you're more comfortable expressing yourself in English or Swahili, Nena meets you where you are with multilingual support that feels natural and culturally sensitive. Built on a robust, scalable cloud infrastructure via Render, Nena is always available when you need someone to listen – day or night.
